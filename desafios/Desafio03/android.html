  • 1.5 - Cupcake
  • 1.6 - Donut
  • 3.0 - Eclair
  • 2.2 - Froyo
  • 2.3 - Gingbread
  • 3.0 - Honeycomb
  • 4.0 - Ice Cream Sandwich
